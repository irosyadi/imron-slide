The even part of a continuous-time signal \(x(t)\), denoted \(\mathcal{E}v\{x(t)\}\), is given by which expression?
A. \( \frac{1}{2}[x(t)+x(-t)] \)
B. \( \frac{1}{2}[x(t)-x(-t)] \)
C. \( x(t) + x(-t) \)
D. \( x(t) / 2 \)
ANSWER: A
The odd part of a continuous-time signal \(x(t)\), denoted \(\mathcal{O}d\{x(t)\}\), is given by which expression?
A. \( \frac{1}{2}[x(t)+x(-t)] \)
B. \( \frac{1}{2}[x(t)-x(-t)] \)
C. \( x(t) - x(-t) \)
D. \( x(t) / 2 \)
ANSWER: B
